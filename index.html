DIVISION 6 LOGO

On August 7th, 2014,the logo I designed for our GIS lab, Division 6, went live!

Represents the CAL FIRE San Luis Obispo Unit's Pre-Fire Division

This is the division under which SLU's GIs happens!

The logo was inspired by the reauleaux triangle,

which is deriveed from three intertwined circles.

The colors are the same RGB's as the CAL FIRE logo.
