DIVISION 6 LOGO

I was also given the opportunity to design the new logo for Division 6

The logo represents CAL FIRE SLU's Pre-Fire Division

Our shop, Pre-Fire Planning and GIS, falls under Division 6.

Divsion 6 also includes Planning and Engineering and Vegetation Management.

The logo was inspired by the reauleaux triangle,

which is deriveed from three intertwined circles.
